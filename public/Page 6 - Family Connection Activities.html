Home Practice Ideas

Mindfulness Moments

  • Practice belly breathing together before meals
  • Share daily gratitude at bedtime
  • Take mindful walks noticing senses

Connection Rituals

  • Weekly family meetings to check in
  • Create an "appreciation notes" jar
  • Establish tech-free conversation times

Problem-Solving Together

  • Tackle a family issue (e.g., chores) using a problem-solving framework
  • Create a 'calm down corner' at home
  • Role-play a tough conversation

Building Empathy

  • Volunteer or do a kindness project
  • Read stories from diverse perspectives
  • Ask, "How do you think they felt?"
