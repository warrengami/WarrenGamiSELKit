What is PromptDJ?

PromptDJ is an AI-powered music generation tool that creates real-time ambient music using MIDI controllers and text prompts. It's perfect for creating calming, engaging, or energizing music to enhance your SEL activities.

Key Features

  • Real-time Generation: Music is created instantly as you adjust controls
  • MIDI Controller Support: Use physical controllers for hands-on music creation
  • Text Prompts: Describe the mood or style you want
  • Multiple Styles: From calm meditation to energetic celebration
  • Volume Control: Adjust levels to suit your classroom environment

How to Use PromptDJ

  1. Launch PromptDJ: Click the "Launch PromptDJ" button from the dashboard
  2. Choose Your Activity: Select from mindfulness, emotion expression, relaxation, or group activities
  3. Adjust Prompts: Modify the text prompts to match your specific SEL activity
  4. Control Music: Use the MIDI controller or on-screen controls to adjust the music
  5. Play/Pause: Start and stop the music as needed during your activity
  6. Volume Management: Keep the volume at an appropriate level for your classroom
üí° Tip: Start with lower volume and gradually increase to find the right level for your activity. The music should enhance, not distract from, the SEL learning.

Classroom Implementation Tips

Before the Activity

  • Test the music volume and quality before class
  • Prepare your prompts based on the SEL activity
  • Have a backup plan in case of technical issues
  • Consider the acoustics of your classroom

During the Activity

  • Start with gentle, calming music for mindfulness activities
  • Use more energetic music for group discussions or celebrations
  • Adjust the mood based on student engagement and energy levels
  • Be prepared to pause or change the music if it's not working

After the Activity

  • Discuss how the music affected the activity
  • Ask students about their emotional responses to different music
  • Reflect on how music can support emotional regulation
  • Consider how to incorporate music in future SEL activities
