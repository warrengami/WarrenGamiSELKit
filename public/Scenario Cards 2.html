Scenario Cards

Click a card to flip it over for guiding questions.

Scenario 7

Your friend made a mistake in a group project. How can you show empathy?

Guiding Questions
  • Instead of blaming, what's a constructive thing to say?
  • How can you work together to fix the mistake?
Scenario 8

You didn't get picked for a team. How might you handle your feelings?

Guiding Questions
  • What emotion are you feeling? (e.g., disappointment, sadness)
  • What is a healthy way to cope with that feeling?
Scenario 9

You notice a classmate looking sad. What could you say or do?

Guiding Questions
  • What are some quiet ways to show you care?
  • What if they don't want to talk?
Scenario 10

A friend shares a secret with you. How do you show you are trustworthy?

Guiding Questions
  • Why is trust important in a friendship?
  • What could happen if you broke their trust?
Scenario 11

You forgot your homework. How can you take responsibility?

Guiding Questions
  • What is the first step you should take?
  • What can you do to make sure it doesn't happen again?
Scenario 12

You're feeling frustrated. What's a healthy way to cope?

Guiding Questions
  • What does "frustrated" feel like in your body?
  • Name one calming strategy you could use.
