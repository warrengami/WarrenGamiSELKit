Scenario Cards

Click a card to flip it over for guiding questions.

Scenario 1

Jordan loses a game and gets upset. What could Alex do to be a good friend?

Guiding Questions
  • How do you think Jordan is feeling?
  • What's one thing Alex could say?
  • What's something Alex should NOT do?
Scenario 2

A classmate forgets their lunch. What are some kind ways to help?

Guiding Questions
  • Why is it important to help others?
  • What could you do if you didn't have extra food to share?
Scenario 3

You see someone sitting alone at recess. What could you do?

Guiding Questions
  • How might that person be feeling?
  • What's a simple, low-pressure way to invite them to play?
Scenario 4

Your friend is nervous about a presentation. How can you support them?

Guiding Questions
  • What are some encouraging words you could say beforehand?
  • How can you be a good audience member for them?
Scenario 5

You and your friend both want the same toy. What's a fair way to solve it?

Guiding Questions
  • What does "compromise" mean?
  • Can you brainstorm two different fair solutions?
Scenario 6

Someone accidentally bumps into you in the hallway. What's the kind thing to do?

Guiding Questions
  • Why is it better to be kind than to get angry in this situation?
  • What could you say to show it's okay?
