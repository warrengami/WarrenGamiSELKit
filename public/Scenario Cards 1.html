Scenario Cards

Social Scenarios for Discussion

Click or press Enter/Space to flip each card and see guiding questions

Scenario 1

A classmate asks to borrow your pencil, but you only have one. What do you do?

Guiding Questions
  • How can you be helpful while also taking care of yourself?
  • What are some other ways you could help your classmate?
Scenario 2

You see someone sitting alone at lunch. How can you include them?

Guiding Questions
  • What might make someone feel welcome?
  • How does it feel to be included?
Scenario 3

A friend tells you a secret. What should you do?

Guiding Questions
  • When is it okay to share someone's secret?
  • How do you show someone you can be trusted?
Scenario 4

You accidentally break something that belongs to someone else. What do you do?

Guiding Questions
  • Why is it important to tell the truth?
  • How can you make things right?
Scenario 5

You disagree with a friend about which game to play. How do you solve this?

Guiding Questions
  • How can you compromise?
  • What makes a good solution for everyone?
Scenario 6

Someone accidentally bumps into you in the hallway. What's the kind thing to do?

Guiding Questions
  • Why is it better to be kind than to get angry in this situation?
  • What could you say to show it's okay?
