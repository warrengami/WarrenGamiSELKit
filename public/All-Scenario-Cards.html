All Scenario Cards

Social-Emotional Learning Scenarios for Discussion

Click each card to flip it and see guiding questions

Scenario 1

Your friends suggest skipping class to hang out in the hallway. You feel torn about what to do. How would you handle this situation?

Guiding Questions
  • What factors would you consider before making your decision?
  • How might your choice affect your relationships and your future?
  • What values are important to you in this situation?
Scenario 2

You're feeling really stressed about a big test. How do you manage these feelings?

Guiding Questions
  • How does stress feel in your body and mind?
  • What strategies help you calm down when you're overwhelmed?
  • Who could you talk to for support?
Scenario 3

You're feeling really anxious about a presentation. How do you cope with these feelings?

Guiding Questions
  • How does anxiety feel in your body?
  • What strategies help you feel more confident?
  • How can you prepare yourself mentally for the challenge?
Scenario 4

You're feeling really angry at a friend. How do you express your feelings without hurting the relationship?

Guiding Questions
  • What are some words or actions that can help communicate anger without hurting someone?
  • How can you take a moment to calm down before responding?
  • What might help you both understand each other's perspective?
Scenario 5

You have to choose between spending time with friends and doing homework. How do you decide?

Guiding Questions
  • What factors would you consider when making this decision?
  • How might your choice affect your future?
  • What would be a responsible way to balance both?
Scenario 6

You're feeling really frustrated with a difficult assignment. How do you manage these feelings?

Guiding Questions
  • How does frustration feel in your body?
  • What strategies help you stay calm when things are challenging?
  • When is it okay to ask for help?
Scenario 7

You see someone being teased in the hallway. What could you do to help?

Guiding Questions
  • How might the person being teased be feeling?
  • What are some safe ways you could intervene?
  • When might you need to get help from an adult?
Scenario 8

You see someone posting mean comments about a classmate online. What do you do?

Guiding Questions
  • How might this affect the person being talked about?
  • What are some positive ways you could respond?
  • How can you be a good digital citizen?
Scenario 9

You see someone being excluded from a group activity. What could you do to help?

Guiding Questions
  • How might the person being excluded be feeling?
  • What are some kind ways you could include them?
  • How might your actions affect the group dynamic?
Scenario 10

You and a friend have a disagreement. How do you express your feelings without making things worse?

Guiding Questions
  • What's a good way to tell someone how you feel without starting a bigger fight?
  • How can you listen to their side of the story?
  • What might help you both find a solution?
Scenario 11

A friend shares a secret with you. How do you show you are trustworthy?

Guiding Questions
  • Why is trust important in a friendship?
  • What could happen if you broke their trust?
  • How can you demonstrate that you're a good friend?
Scenario 12

You and a friend have different opinions about something important. How do you handle this?

Guiding Questions
  • How can you respect their opinion while still sharing your own?
  • What does it mean to 'agree to disagree'?
  • How can you maintain your friendship despite the disagreement?
Scenario 13

You forgot your homework. How can you take responsibility?

Guiding Questions
  • What is the first step you should take?
  • What can you do to make sure it doesn't happen again?
  • How can you be honest about the situation?
Scenario 14

You're about to post something online about a friend. How do you decide if it's appropriate?

Guiding Questions
  • What should you consider before posting something about someone else?
  • How might your post affect your friend's feelings?
  • What makes something appropriate or inappropriate to share online?
